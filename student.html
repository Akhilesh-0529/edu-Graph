Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is a noun phrase?

A: A noun phrase is a group of words built around a noun (e.g., "the big brown dog") that functions collectively as a subject, object, or prepositional object in a sentence.

Q: What is the difference between active and passive voice?

A: In the active voice, the subject performs the action (e.g., "The chef prepared the meal"). In the passive voice, the subject receives the action (e.g., "The meal was prepared by the chef").
